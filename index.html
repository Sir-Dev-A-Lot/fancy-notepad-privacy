Fancy Notepad — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-11

Fancy Notepad is developed by Sir-Dev-A-Lot. We respect your privacy and design this app to keep your data on your device.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers.

  • Notes & content: stored locally on your device (IndexedDB or native storage when installed as an APK).
  • Preferences: theme, font, sort order, and optional app PIN are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The current version includes no analytics SDKs and no advertising SDKs.

If we later integrate billing or analytics, we’ll update this policy and our Data Safety form accordingly.

Permissions

  • Storage (optional): used only when you import/export notes you choose to save as files.
  • Network: used to load web assets initially and for optional share/export features. The app works offline after installation.

Children’s Privacy

Fancy Notepad is intended for users aged 13 and older.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
